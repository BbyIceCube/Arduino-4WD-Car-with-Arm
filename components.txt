Here are the components

-Dc to Dc step down buck converter
-L298N Motor driver
-4x Gear motors
-4x Servo motors
-2x 3.7V Lithium ion Batteries for car
-4x 1.5V AAA Batteries for Arm 
-Ultrasonic Sensor
-HC-05 Bluetooth module
-Arduino UNO

-I've done this project as a leader of 20 members group with 2 co-leader. And this is my first project of University.
